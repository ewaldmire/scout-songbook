\skittitle{The Gum Tree}

From: https://moorecountyboyscouts.org/uniform_pdf/100_skits.pdf

Characters: 3+ Scouts\\

Scout \#1: Calls up a Den leader or Cubmaster to pose as their "Tree"\\

Scout \#1: walks up to the "tree" chewing gum and says and "man, this is some tasty gum, I think I'll save it for later!" (sticks it to the "tree")\\

Scout \#2: walks up to the "tree" yawning / stretching then lean on the tree and say and "EWWW Gum!" and throws the gum on the ground.\\

Scout \#3: (mime-ing bouncing a basketball and walking with another scout) says "Thanks for playing basketball with me" (then stops looks at the ball) and says "EWWW Gum! (throws it on the ground)\\

Scout \#4: skips out to the "tree" and stops to look at their shoe and says "Gross!  Gum!  I'll stick it to this tree so no one else steps in it"\\

Scout \#1: walks back out and says "Yay!  My gum is still here!" and puts it in their mouth...\\
